# **App Name**: Bengal Vista

## Core Features:

- Cultural Facts Display: Browse and view detailed cultural facts about Bangladesh, each accompanied by a relevant emoji.
- Traditional Recipes View: Access and display a collection of traditional Bangladeshi food recipes, including ingredients and preparation steps.
- Tabbed Page Navigation: Effortlessly switch between the 'Interesting Facts' and 'Foods Prep' pages using a clear tab-based interface.
- Responsive Layout Adaptation: Ensure the application's layout dynamically adjusts to provide an optimal viewing experience across all device sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop).
- AI Insight Tool: An AI tool that provides a deeper dive into selected facts or recipes, generating supplementary details, rephrased summaries, or contextual information upon user request.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Red (#E0444C), symbolizing the flag of Bangladesh, used for headings, key interactive elements, and accents.
- Secondary color: Lush Green (#006A4E), representing the landscape and prosperity of Bangladesh, used for supporting elements and interactive highlights.
- Background color: Soft Off-White (#FDFDFD), ensuring a clean and neutral canvas that allows the vibrant red and green to stand out.
- Text color: Dark Gray (#333333) for readability against the light background.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clean lines and excellent readability across all content types.
- Utilize the provided emojis as integrated design elements to categorize and enhance the readability and charm of fact and recipe listings.
- Clean, structured card-based layouts for individual facts and recipes, facilitating easy digestion of information while maintaining elegance. Pages will feature clear tab-based navigation.
- Subtle and smooth transition animations for tab switching and card interactions to provide a fluid and polished user experience.
